Why are predictions sometimes wrong? (Model limitations)

This neural network utilizes a pre-trained MobileNetV2 model augmented with custom waste classification datasets. While highly robust, it can still be affected by certain conditions:

  • Complex Backgrounds: A colorful bag on a brown wooden desk or table may be confused for cardboard due to background color bleed. Use the drag & zoom cropper to isolate the object!
  • Material Ambiguity: Colorful plastic/foil chip packets have highly reflective, shiny surfaces. The network can confuse these specular highlights with the metallic textures of tin and aluminum cans, predicting metal.
  • Transparency: Clear plastic water bottles show the background table color. If on a wood surface, the brown background bleed can lead to misclassification as cardboard.
